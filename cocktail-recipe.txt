Gibson recipe

serve in
Cocktail Glass
alcohol
16%

servings Go
1 1/2 oz gin
3/4 oz vermouth
2 cocktail onions

Stir gin and vermouth over ice cubes in a mixing glass. Strain into a cocktail glass. Add the cocktail onions and serve.
